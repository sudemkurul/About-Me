About Me

I am a first-year political science and public administration student at Istanbul Medipol University with a strong passion for history, politics, law, and cultural anthropology. My academic journey is focused on exploring how political systems, legal frameworks, and social structures shape societies worldwide.

Over the past few years, I have expanded my knowledge through intensive academic research and by completing various online courses from leading institutions such as Cambridge, Edinburgh, Yale, and Harvard. These courses have strengthened my understanding of global politics, legal theory, and public policy.

1) Projects & Research

Beyond my academic studies, I work on independent projects—including blogs and websites—where I publish essays, book analyses, and research on history, politics, and law. These platforms let me combine academic insights with personal interests and share my work with a broader audience.

2) Academic Engagements & Activities

  • Attended political science and law conferences organized at Istanbul Medipol University.
  • Participated in a panel discussion on human rights, democracy, and constitutional law.
  • Completed a research-oriented essay project on history, anthropology, and political theory.
  • Engaged in critical debates and presentations on international governance and global security.

3) Volunteering & Social Projects

  • UNICEF Youth Network — Actively supported campaigns by collecting signatures for animal rights and raising awareness on environmental and social issues.
  • TEMA Foundation — Participated in environmental projects and tree-planting activities.
  • TBMM (Turkish Grand National Assembly) Visit — Observed parliamentary processes and gained insight into legislative systems.
  • 15 July Democracy Rallies — Participated in events commemorating democratic values and civic resilience.

4) Upcoming Memberships & Future Goals

  • National Geographic Volunteer Program (upcoming) — Environmental and educational initiatives.
  • The Royal Historical Society (UK) (planned) — Connecting with historians and publishing research on global history.
  • British Political Students Association (planned) — Discussions on public policy and governance.
  • European Council on Foreign Relations (ECFR) (planned) — Contributing to policy dialogues on diplomacy and security.

5) Vision & Aspirations

My goal is to integrate my academic studies, personal projects, and volunteering experiences to build a strong interdisciplinary background in politics, history, and law. I aim to contribute to research on global governance, human rights, and international policy-making while sharing my findings through writing, conferences, and collaborative projects.

Thank you for visiting my page! I will continue to share my research, essays, and upcoming initiatives here.
